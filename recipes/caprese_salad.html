Caprese Salad

Grab a ball of fresh mozzarella, a handful of basil, extra virgin olive oil and red ripen tomatoes and make this delicious Caprese salad!

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 medium tomatoes, sliced
  • 8-ounce ball fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • sea salt and ground pepper
  • optional: balsamic vinegar (for drizzling)

Steps:

  1. Arrange the tomatoes, mozzarella and basil leaves in a platter. You can arrange them in layers or mix them up!
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with sea salt and ground pepper.
  3. If desired, drizzle the balsamic vinegar on top.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
