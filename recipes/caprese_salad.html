Caprese Salad

Grab a ball of fresh mozzarella, a handful of basil, extra virgin olive oil and red ripen tomatoes and make this delicious Caprese salad!

Caprese salad is a vibrant and refreshing Italian dish that celebrates the simplicity of fresh, high-quality ingredients.

This classic salad typically features ripe, juicy tomatoes, creamy mozzarella cheese, and fragrant basil leaves, all drizzled with a splash of extra virgin olive oil and a sprinkle of salt and pepper.

Often enhanced with a touch of balsamic vinegar or a few pine nuts, Caprese salad is not just a feast for the palate but also a visually appealing dish, embodying the essence of Mediterranean cuisine with its colorful presentation and wholesome ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 3-4 medium tomatoes, sliced
  • 8-ounce ball fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • sea salt and ground pepper
  • optional: balsamic vinegar (for drizzling)

Steps:

  1. Arrange the tomatoes, mozzarella and basil leaves in a platter. You can arrange them in layers or mix them up!
  2. Drizzle with olive oil and sprinkle with sea salt and ground pepper.
  3. If desired, drizzle the balsamic vinegar on top.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
