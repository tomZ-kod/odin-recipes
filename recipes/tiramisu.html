Tiramisu

What could be better than a sweet and creamy tiramisu to end the day?

Tiramisu is a decadent Italian dessert renowned for its rich, layered flavors and creamy texture.

Traditionally topped with a dusting of cocoa powder, tiramisu presents a delightful interplay of coffee and cream, creating a dessert that's both elegant and comforting.

Its harmonious blend of flavors and textures has made it a beloved favorite in both casual and fine dining settings, embodying the art of Italian dessert-making.

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups heavy whipping cream
  • 8 ounce mascarpone cheese
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1.5 cups cold espresso
  • 3 tablespoons coffee flavored liqueur
  • 1 package LadyFingers
  • Cocoa powder

Steps:

  1. Add whipping cream to a mixing bowl and beat on a medium speed. Slowly add sugar.
  2. Add mascarpone cheese to the whipping cream and folder until combined.
  3. On a separate bowl, add coffee and liqueur to a shallow bowl. Dip the lady fingers in the coffee.
  4. Lay the lady fingers in a single layer on the bottom of an 8x8" pan.
  5. Smooth half of the mascarpone mixture over the lady fingers.
  6. Add another layer of dipped lady fingers. Add a final layer of remaining mascarpone mixture.
  7. Dust cocoa powder on top. Refrigerate for 3-4 hours.
