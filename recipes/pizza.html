Margherita Pizza

Roll up your sleeves and get ready to make the best homemade pizza!

Pizza is one of the most universally adored comfort foods, celebrated for its versatility and deliciousness.

At its core, pizza features a crispy, golden crust topped with a rich tomato sauce and gooey melted cheese.

From there, the possibilities are endless: you can opt for classic toppings like pepperoni and mushrooms or get adventurous with ingredients like goat cheese, figs, or truffle oil.

Whether you prefer a thin, crispy New York-style slice or a deep-dish Chicago-style pie loaded with layers of toppings, pizza brings people together, often becoming the centerpiece of gatherings and a canvas for culinary creativity.

Its ability to adapt to regional tastes and personal preferences makes it a beloved staple in cuisines around the world.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Fresh pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 8 ounces fresh mozzarella, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
  • 10 fresh basil leaves
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Optional: Red pepper flakes (for extra spice)

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 400F (or 205C).
  2. Using a rolling pan, roll dough to desired thickness.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce on the dough.
  4. Top with fresh mozzarella and tomatoes.
  5. Bake for 10 to 12 minutes, or until the crust is browned.
  6. Remove from oven and top with fresh basil and a pinch of oregano.
  7. Optional: top with a pinch of red pepper flakes for extra spice
